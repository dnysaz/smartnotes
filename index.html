Your Data, Your Control

Smart Note is a client-side application. We do not have servers to store your notes, tasks, or financial records. Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to sync it to your Google Drive.

Privacy Architecture:

  • Data is stored in LocalStorage by default.
  • Google Sync uses your private app-data folder.

Clearing your browser cache will delete local data. Always sync to Google Drive for safety.

Questions? Visit our GitHub Issues page.

Help & Support

Google Drive Sync

Go to Settings → Cloud Services → tap Google Drive Sync to connect. Once connected, your data is automatically saved to your private Google Drive folder. You can also tap it to sync manually anytime.

Sync runs automatically every 30 seconds when you're connected, so changes from other devices show up in real time.

Without Sync (Guest Mode)

All data stays only on this device and may be lost if you clear browser data. You can connect to Google Drive anytime from Settings without losing your notes. Signing out as guest will permanently delete all local data.

Finance Tracking

Create "Cash Books" for different budgets like Travel, Monthly, or Work. Track income and expenses in Indonesian Rupiah (or other currencies) with automatic totals.
